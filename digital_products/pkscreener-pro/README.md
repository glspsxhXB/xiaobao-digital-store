# PKScreener Pro

Professional version of PKScreener. A Python-based stock screener for NSE, India. PKScreener is an advanced free stock screener to find potential breakout stocks from NSE and show its possible breakout values. It also helps to find the stocks which are consolidating and may breakout, or the particular chart patterns that you're looking specifically to make your decisions.
